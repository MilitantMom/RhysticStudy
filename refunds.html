Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping with Rhystic Studios. We strive to ensure that you are satisfied with your purchase. If you are not fully satisfied, please review our refund policy below:

Eligibility for Refund

Refunds may be issued under the following conditions:

  • The product is defective or damaged upon delivery.
  • You received an incorrect product or service.
  • The product or service was purchased in error or with technical issues.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team within 14 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order details and a description of the issue.
  3. Our team will review the request and respond within 2-3 business days.

Refund Processing

If your refund is approved, it will be processed back to the original payment method. Please note that it may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software or digital products after purchase
  • Any items marked as final sale

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please feel free to contact our customer support team at support@rhysticstudio.com.
